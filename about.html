About Us

At Rise & Shine, we're not just bakers; we're passionate creators, dedicated to bringing a touch of sweetness to your day. Proudly located in the vibrant Cairns community, we are excited to serve up a delightful array of freshly baked goods that embody our commitment to quality, flavor, and a warm, friendly atmosphere. Join us on this mouthwatering journey, and let Rise & Shine Bakery become your local destination for all things sweet and satisfying.
